## Business Goal

Build a scalable, SaaS-ready HRMS portal as a product for the company.

The platform should start with a lean and commercially sensible MVP, then expand phase by phase into a broader HR platform.

## Product Direction

Use Info-Tech HRMS as inspiration and benchmark reference for overall direction and module coverage only.

Do not blindly copy the benchmark product and do not place the full module set into the first release.

## Current Product Owner Task

Define and deliver the HRMS product in phased releases, starting with a focused MVP that forms the foundation for future expansion.

## MVP Intent

The first release should focus on the smallest sensible set of HRMS capabilities that can support product validation and future SaaS scaling.

Current MVP direction:

- employee records and organization structure
- role-based access
- employee and manager self-service portal
- leave management
- basic attendance handling
- approvals
- basic dashboards and operational reporting

## Out of Scope for Initial MVP

- full benchmark module parity
- blind feature copying from Info-Tech
- non-essential advanced modules in the first release
- large later-phase capabilities such as advanced payroll, ATS, LMS, appraisal, project costing, and other extended HR platform modules unless later approved
